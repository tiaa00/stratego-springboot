Stratego is a two player board game for 2 players that was invented by the French military officer and chess master . The object of this strategy warfare game is to capture your opponent's flag while protecting yours from being captured.

In order to win you must either: Capture all enemy pieces or block them so they cannot move ; Or kill an opposing piece with one of your own (this can be done in any number of ways). If both sides have no more moves then it's considered stalemate!
